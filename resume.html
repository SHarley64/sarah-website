Hey y'all! Im Sarah Harley, I am a designer, illustrator, muralist, and small business owner with West Kootenay roots. My work is inspired by the natural world, drawing inspiration from a juxtaposition between nature and supernatural colours. I am an adventurer, with a kindhearted outside (the box) visual storytelling approach who is versatile in all terrain and can translate those skills across all formats. I love to create work that makes us feel, think, and smile like we mean it while also encouraging us to have an appreciation for nature, connection, and community.
